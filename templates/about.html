Welcome to PlantGuard, a plant leaf disease recognition system. The website harnesses the power of Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) algorithm to detect plant conditions. Simply upload a photo of a plant leaf and get the status almost immediately, whether it is healthy, affected by powdery mildew, or rust. With PlantGuard, you can keep your plants thriving and disease-free with ease.

** The system is not 100% accurate yet and may occasionally detect the most prominent condition it identifies.

Healthy

A healthy plant is one that is free from disease, with vibrant green leaves, strong stems, and vigorous growth.

To maintain:

  • Ensure proper watering.
  • Provide adequate sunlight
  • Maintain proper nutrition
  • Monitor for pests and diseases

Powdery

Powdery mildew is a fungal disease that appears as a white or gray powdery coating. It is caused by various species of fungi.

Remedies:

  • Prune affected plant parts
  • Improve air circulation
  • Apply fungicides
  • Water at the base of the plant

Rust

Rust is a fungal disease that appears as orange or reddish-brown spots. It is caused by various species of fungi.

Remedies:

  • Remove affected plant parts
  • Improve air circulation
  • Apply fungicides
  • Practice good garden hygiene
